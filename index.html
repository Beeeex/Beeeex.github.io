About me

My name is Massimiliano Bassan, I'm a web developer,graphic and video editor from Italy.
I am a technology enthusiast who loves team work. Hardworking, self-taught and curious, I like to see my projects come to life.

Since I was a child I've been fascinated by the world. I got my first personal computer when I was 5 years old.
I discovered HTML when I was 13 and I have been using Photoshop and Premiere since the age of 15.
I feel curious about everything, passionate about creating and discovering my skills.
I enjoy exploring new languages, meeting new people, conceiving websites, achieving new projects, and much more.
That's what motivates me in my daily work.
